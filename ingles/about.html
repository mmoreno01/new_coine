We Specialize in :

The International Business Council (COINE) guides you throughout the business process, from identifying the opportunity, to closing deals and follow them up to ensure continuity. More than 20 years of experience in the legal, fiscal, accounting and financial areas support us, providing companies with the required advice for the successful materialization of a business in Mexico and abroad together with our international alliances.
We have the experience and methodologies with which we can internationalize companies or projects by approaching local authorities and the target market, as well as generating links with national and international counterparts, aimed to generate confidence and clarity at the moment of starting operations in other countries.

In addition, our experience allows us to strengthen the technical business processes of companies, allowing a successful internationalization.
COINE’s business network allows to find the appropriate counterparts for the diversification of clients and markets. For this, COINE carries out studies and analysis that generate the necessary intelligence to select those counterparts with which the companies might have real business opportunities in the short, medium and long term. In addition, we provide necessary advice for the correct technical evaluation to achieve an effective closure.
COINE has a multidisciplinary platform of experts in different areas: tax, legal, accounting, audit and financial, as well as a network of international offices with a global presence, to give the necessary boost to those projects in the initial stage or in development. In addition, we have a methodology to strengthen projects with high profitability potential, through which we intend to support young entrepreneurs through specialized consulting and access to equity.
COINE’s platform includes financing or investment institutions that are linked to the projects and companies with which we work in the Council. To do this, we help companies to strengthen their administrative and management activities, including the implementation of corporate governments to be able to access investment equity or financing more easily.
Business development refers to the multidisciplinary management that COINE is able to generate, for the definition of products and services, aimed at strengthening its commercialization. Definition of new opportunities, creation of needs and design of an alliance strategy. Let's say that business development goes ahead of the rest of the areas defining the strategies for business progress, taking advantage of the opportunities of the environment or creating them, as well as establishing the necessary synergies with external entities.
The International Business Council COINE established an educational strategy with the goal of creating alliances with higher education institutions and synergy between entrepreneurs and companies where the need of a company meets the talent and innovation of young entrepreneurs to support them and promote internationalization of success stories.
