COINE STANDS OUT FOR GENERATING NATIONAL AND INTERNATIONAL LINKS

The International Business Council (COINE) stands out for generating high-value national and international links by approaching companies, investors, business chambers, governments and academic institutions. The foregoing is supported by a business viability analysis, as well as by our model of effective engagement, professional business support and the continuous success stories.

We also have global presence through professionals within a 176 offices and national and international firms network, which makes us a specialized board in the internationalization of businesses and a partner with great potential for closing business opportunities that boost the economic development of the regions.

We Specialize in :

The International Business Council COINE assists you throughout the business process, from identifying opportunities to the closing and follow-up to ensure continuity. 20 years of experience support us in the legal, tax, accounting and financial areas, which provide businesses the required advice for the successful materialization of a business in Mexico and abroad along with their international alliances, with which we can generate approaches with local authorities to generate confidence and clearness at the time of starting operations in other countries.
COINE has a specialized multidisciplinary platform of experts in different areas such as: tax, legal, accounting, auditing and financial, as well as a network of international offices with a presence in the 5 continents, to boost those projects in the initial stage or provide them with development that they require.

In addition, we have an incubation and acceleration platform for high profitability potential, projects through which we intend to support young entrepreneurs through specialized consulting and access to equity.
The International Business Council COINE established an Investment Fund so that in addition to raising funds, education is also boosted, aiming to provide support to young entrepreneurs, as well as the internationalization of talent.
