About Us

The International Business Council of Latin America and the Caribbean (COINE), is constituted as the governing body of the economic boost and linkage of Latin America and the Caribbean area, integrating itself in the same way in Asia and Africa through alliances with the International Conference of Asian Political Parties (ICAPP) and Council of African Political Parties (CAPP)..

Our objective is to contribute to the development of political and economic integration among countries of the Latin American region. We have a perfectly established structure that allows the facilitation of business agreements and alliances, whose scope has a beneficial impact on economies and society as a whole.
